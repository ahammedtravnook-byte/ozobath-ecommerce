### Frequently Asked Questions About BathStory
[Does BathStory do full bathroom renovations?](https://bathstory.in#t4s-accor_item_ULqthm)
No. BathStory does not offer full bathroom renovations. We specialise in shower enclosures, floor drains, smart LED mirrors, and bathroom fittings, with professional installation support for shower enclosures only.
[Which cities does BathStory install shower enclosures in?](https://bathstory.in#t4s-accor_item_NRhLez)
BathStory currently provides shower enclosure installation in Bangalore and Hyderabad.
All other BathStory products such as floor drains, shower channels, LED mirrors, and bathroom accessories can be ordered online and delivered pan India.
[How long does BathStory take to install a shower enclosure?](https://bathstory.in#t4s-accor_item_c4fCg8)
After measurements are finalised, BathStory typically installs shower enclosures within 7 business days in Bangalore and 10-12 business days in Hyderabad, depending on the project.
[Does BathStory require a site visit before booking a shower enclosure?](https://bathstory.in#t4s-accor_item_KQb7WR)
A site visit is recommended to ensure accurate measurements and to select the right enclosure type. BathStory also offers assisted options such as talking to expert team, visiting an experience centre or shopping on live video call.
[What warranty does BathStory provide on shower enclosures?](https://bathstory.in#t4s-accor_item_PTnaCE)
BathStory provides a 7 year warranty on hardware and a 3 year warranty on glass for shower enclosures, subject to standard usage and care conditions.
[Can I buy BathStory products without a site visit?](https://bathstory.in#t4s-accor_item_7kGxbW)
Yes. BathStory products such as floor drains, shower channels, smart LED mirrors, and bathroom accessories can be purchased directly online without a site visit and are delivered across India.
[Are BathStory products designed for Indian bathrooms?](https://bathstory.in#t4s-accor_item_baHWqA)
Yes. BathStory products are thoughtfully designed for Indian bathroom layouts, water conditions, and daily usage, with a focus on function, durability, and clean aesthetics.
[Can I visit BathStory products in person before buying?](https://bathstory.in#t4s-accor_item_Xb7fM7)
Yes. You can visit a BathStory experience centre to view shower enclosures and other bathroom products before making a decision.
